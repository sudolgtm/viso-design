About Us

What We Do

We are a trusted West Kelowna plumbing and HVAC company specializing in new construction for commercial, industrial, and residential projects across the Okanagan.

Leask & Company has been serving Kelowna and the surrounding areas for years, delivering expert plumbing, heating, and air conditioning solutions. As a locally owned and family-run business, we take pride in our workmanship and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our skilled technicians are equipped with the latest tools and knowledge to handle projects of any scale — from residential repairs to large commercial and industrial installations.

Our Services

Expert Plumbing, Heating & AC

From emergency repairs to full-system installations, we handle all your mechanical needs with professionalism and care.

Plumbing Services

Complete plumbing solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial properties. Our journeyman plumbers deliver quality workmanship on every project.

  • New construction plumbing installation
  • Fixture replacement & repair (sinks, toilets, faucets)
  • Poly-B pipe replacement & re-piping
  • Water heater installation & maintenance

Heating Services

Keep your home or business warm and comfortable year-round with our professional heating services, from furnace repairs to full system installations.

  • Furnace repair, maintenance & installation
  • Heat pump installation & servicing
  • Gas fitting & gas line installation
  • Radiant & hydronic heating systems

Air Conditioning

Stay cool during Okanagan summers with our expert AC services. We install, maintain, and repair a wide range of air conditioning systems for any property.

  • AC installation for residential & commercial
  • Ductless mini-split systems
  • Air conditioning repair & maintenance
  • Air exchanger & ventilation systems
