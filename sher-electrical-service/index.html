What We Do

Our Electrical Services

Professional electrical solutions for your home, from minor repairs to full installations.

Electrical Repairs & Troubleshooting

Fast, reliable diagnosis and repair for all types of electrical issues in your home. From flickering lights to faulty outlets, we get things working safely and efficiently.

  • Diagnose and repair faulty wiring and circuits
  • Outlet, switch, and receptacle replacement
  • Breaker and fuse troubleshooting
  • Emergency service available

Lighting Installation & Design

Transform your home with professional lighting solutions. We handle everything from simple fixture swaps to complete custom lighting designs for any room.

  • Interior and exterior lighting installation
  • LED upgrade and retrofit solutions
  • Pot lights, chandeliers, and ceiling fans
  • Landscape and security lighting

Electrical Panel Upgrades

Upgrade your electrical panel to handle modern power demands safely. Essential for older homes, new appliances, or adding EV chargers and home additions.

  • 100A, 200A, and 400A panel upgrades
  • Breaker replacement and fuse-to-breaker conversions
  • Subpanel installation for additions and suites
  • Load calculations and code-compliant installation

EV Charger Installation

Go electric with confidence. We install Level 2 EV chargers for homes and businesses, ensuring safe, fast, and code-compliant charging every time.

  • Level 2 charger installation for all EV models
  • Dedicated circuit setup and panel prep
  • Indoor and outdoor charger mounting
  • Permit handling and final inspection

New Construction & Renovation Wiring

Complete wiring solutions for new builds, major renovations, and home additions. We work with builders and homeowners to deliver safe, code-compliant electrical systems.

  • Full rewiring for older homes and renovations
  • New build rough-in and trim-out
  • Dedicated circuits for kitchen, spa, and workshop
  • Smart home and network cabling

Safety Inspections & Maintenance

Keep your home and family safe with thorough electrical inspections and preventative maintenance. Identify potential hazards before they become serious problems.

  • Comprehensive electrical safety inspections
  • Smoke and CO2 alarm installation and testing
  • Surge protection for your entire home
  • Preventive maintenance and code compliance checks
