Our Plumbing Services

Professional plumbing solutions for residential and commercial properties in West Kelowna and the Okanagan.

Re-Piping & Poly B Replacement

Is your home still equipped with Poly B plumbing? We specialize in complete re-piping from Poly B to durable PEX, giving you peace of mind and protecting your home from costly water damage.

  • Full Poly B to PEX repiping
  • Water line replacement and upgrades
  • Leak detection and pipe repair
  • Whole-home plumbing system upgrades

Hot Water Tank & Heater Installation

From traditional hot water tanks to modern tankless systems, we handle all water heater installations, replacements, and repairs. We work with both gas and electric units to keep your hot water flowing.

  • Hot water tank replacement & installation
  • Tankless water heater installation
  • Gas and electric water heater service
  • Water heater maintenance & repairs

Emergency Plumbing & Drain Cleaning

Plumbing emergencies don't wait, and neither do we. Available 24/7, we respond quickly to burst pipes, clogged drains, toilet backups, and any urgent plumbing issue you're facing.

  • 24/7 emergency plumbing service
  • Drain cleaning & clog removal
  • Faucet repair & toilet installation
  • Kitchen & bathroom plumbing repairs
