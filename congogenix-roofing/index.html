About Us

What We Do

CongoGenix Roofing is a locally owned, family-operated roofing company serving Lethbridge and surrounding areas. With over 30 years of combined experience, we specialize in SBS torch-on flat roofing and architectural asphalt shingle installation and repairs. We are dedicated to safe work practices while providing exceptional quality at very affordable prices.

Our Services

Professional Roofing Services

From complete roof replacements to targeted repairs, we handle all your roofing needs with expert craftsmanship and attention to detail.

SBS Torch-On Flat Roofing

Expert torch-on membrane installation for flat and low-slope roofs. Our SBS-modified bitumen systems provide superior waterproofing and durability for commercial and residential buildings.

  • Professional SBS torch-on membrane installation
  • Complete flat roof replacement and repairs
  • Superior waterproofing and leak protection
  • Durable long-lasting commercial-grade systems

Architectural Asphalt Shingles

Premium architectural asphalt shingle installation that enhances your home's curb appeal while providing exceptional protection against the elements. We use high-quality materials built to last.

  • Premium architectural shingle installation
  • Complete shingle tear-off and disposal
  • New roof construction and re-roofing
  • Thorough cleanup including debris removal

Roof Installation & Repair

Whether you need a complete roof replacement or targeted repairs for storm damage and leaks, our experienced team delivers prompt, professional service with transparent pricing and lasting results.

  • Complete roof installation and replacement
  • Leak detection and emergency roof repairs
  • Storm and wind damage restoration
  • Thorough roof inspections with detailed estimates

Gutters, Soffit & Fascia

Complete exterior water management solutions including 5-inch seamless gutter installation, soffit and fascia replacement. Protect your home's foundation and roofline with our professional services.

  • 5-inch seamless gutter installation
  • Gutter repairs and maintenance
  • Soffit and fascia replacement
  • Complete exterior drainage solutions
