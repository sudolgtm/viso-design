What We Do

Expert Plumbing Services in Kelowna

Limebee Plumbing is a Kelowna-based plumbing company owned and operated by Karl Kral, a licensed plumber with over 12 years of hands-on experience. We provide personalized, high-quality plumbing services for both residential and commercial clients across the Okanagan.

Our fully stocked service truck means we can handle most plumbing issues on the spot — from a clogged drain to a full water heater replacement. We believe in honest pricing, clear communication, and work done right the first time.

Our Services

Full-Service Plumbing Solutions

From minor fixes to major installations, we handle it all with professional care and attention to detail.

Repairs & Drain Cleaning

Fast, effective solutions for all your plumbing repair needs. Whether it's a stubborn clog or a leaky fixture, we'll diagnose the problem and get it fixed.

  • Clogged drain clearing & snaking
  • Sink, faucet & fixture repairs
  • Toilet repair & replacement
  • Dishwasher & appliance hookups

Water Heaters & Gas Services

Keep your hot water flowing and your gas appliances running safely. We install, repair, and maintain all types of water heating systems.

  • Water heater installation & repair
  • Tankless water heater systems
  • Gas line repairs & hookups
  • Water supply to fridges & icemakers

Renovations & New Installations

Upgrade your space with professional plumbing installations. From bathroom remodels to new construction, we bring expertise to every project.

  • Complete bathroom & kitchen renovations
  • New fixture installations & upgrades
  • New construction plumbing
  • Commercial & residential projects

Water Filtration & Systems

Enjoy clean, great-tasting water throughout your home. We design and install custom water filtration solutions tailored to your needs.

  • Whole-home water filter systems
  • Under-sink filtration & reverse osmosis
  • Water softener installation & service
  • Commercial filtration systems
