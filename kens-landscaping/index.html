About Us

What We Do

Ken's Landscaping Ltd is an established company that has been serving Chilliwack and surrounding areas for over 18 years. We provide landscape install and design services, as well as commercial and residential property maintenance. Whether you need a regular schedule maintenance, one-time spruce up, or an emergency storm clean up, we are ready to get the job done.

Professional Landscaping, Every Season

Our team of professionals has the knowledge and experience to make your yard something to be proud of. We take pride in delivering outstanding workmanship with clean trucks, reliable crews, and exceptional results — every single time.

From residential weekly maintenance to new landscape installs, renovations, and commercial property care, we bring the same dedication to every project.

Our Services

Comprehensive Landscaping Services

From design to maintenance, we handle every aspect of your outdoor space with professionalism and care.

Landscape Design

Our expert design team works with you to create a stunning outdoor space that reflects your style and enhances your property. We transform your vision into a detailed plan that sets the foundation for exceptional results.

  • Custom landscape design tailored to your property
  • Hardscape planning and material selection
  • Plant selection suited to Chilliwack's climate
  • 3D concepts and detailed blueprint drawings

Lawn Maintenance

Keep your lawn looking its best all year round with our comprehensive maintenance programs. Whether you need weekly service or a one-time clean-up, our crews deliver consistent, high-quality care.

  • Regular lawn mowing and edging
  • Fertilizing and weed control programs
  • Seasonal clean-up and leaf removal
  • One-time spruce-up and touch-up services

Tree Trimming & Removal

Our tree care services keep your trees healthy, safe, and looking beautiful. From routine trimming to emergency storm clean-up, we handle trees of all sizes with professional equipment and expertise.

  • Professional tree trimming and pruning
  • Safe tree removal and stump grinding
  • Brush removal and lot clearing
  • Emergency storm damage clean-up

Garden Beds & Boxes

Create vibrant garden spaces that add colour, texture, and life to your property. We design and install beautiful garden beds and raised planter boxes that thrive in the Fraser Valley climate.

  • Custom garden bed design and installation
  • Raised planter boxes for vegetables and flowers
  • Mulching, planting, and seasonal rotation
  • Weed control and ongoing bed maintenance

Commercial Landscaping

Enhance your business's curb appeal with professional commercial landscaping. A well-maintained property sends the right message to your customers and creates an inviting atmosphere that sets you apart.

  • Scheduled commercial maintenance plans
  • Landscape design and installation for businesses
  • Snow removal and winter property care
  • Year-round grounds maintenance
