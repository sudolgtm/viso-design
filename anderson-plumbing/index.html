Our Services

Professional plumbing and gas fitting for residential and commercial properties

Plumbing Services

From leaky faucets to full bathroom renovations, we handle all residential and commercial plumbing needs with precision and care.

  • Leak detection and pipe repair
  • Drain cleaning and clog removal
  • Bathroom and kitchen plumbing
  • Commercial plumbing systems

Gas Fitting

Licensed gas fitters for safe, code-compliant installations of gas lines, appliances, and outdoor connections throughout the Okanagan.

  • Gas appliance hookups (stoves, dryers, BBQs)
  • Gas line installation and repair
  • Fire pit and outdoor gas connections
  • Leak detection and safety inspections

Water Heater Services

Expert installation, repair, and maintenance of traditional and tankless water heaters to keep your hot water flowing reliably.

  • Tank and tankless water heater installation
  • Water heater repair and maintenance
  • Furnace inspections and diagnostics
  • PRV replacement and system upgrades

Emergency Plumbing

Available 24/7 for urgent plumbing emergencies. When a pipe bursts or a leak won't wait, we respond fast to minimize damage.

  • 24/7 emergency response
  • Burst pipe repair and water damage control
  • Emergency gas leak detection
  • After-hours and weekend service
