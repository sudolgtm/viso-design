Our Services

Comprehensive pressure washing solutions tailored to your property's needs. We use professional equipment and eco-friendly techniques for outstanding results.

Driveway & Concrete Cleaning

Restore your driveway, walkways, and concrete surfaces to like-new condition. Our high-pressure system removes years of dirt, oil stains, moss, and grime in one visit.

  • Driveway and parking area cleaning
  • Walkway and sidewalk restoration
  • Oil stain and grime removal
  • Moss, algae, and mildew treatment

Residential Pressure Washing

Enhance your home's curb appeal with a thorough exterior clean. We gently yet effectively clean siding, decks, fences, patios, and more — making your home look freshly maintained.

  • House siding and exterior washing
  • Deck, patio, and fence cleaning
  • Roof and gutter cleaning
  • Driveway and garage floor cleaning

Commercial Pressure Washing

Make a great first impression with a clean commercial property. We serve businesses throughout Kelowna, keeping storefronts, parking lots, and building exteriors spotless.
