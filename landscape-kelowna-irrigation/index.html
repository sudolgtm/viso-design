What We Do

Landscape Kelowna Maintenance & Irrigation is your trusted partner for creating and maintaining stunning outdoor spaces in Kelowna, British Columbia. We specialize in transforming ordinary properties into beautiful, functional landscapes that you can enjoy year-round.

From routine lawn care to custom landscape design and efficient irrigation systems, our team delivers quality craftsmanship with honest pricing and meticulous attention to detail on every project.

Our Services

Comprehensive landscaping solutions tailored to your property's unique needs

Lawn Maintenance

Keep your lawn looking its best all season long with our comprehensive maintenance services. We take care of every detail so your property always looks pristine.

  • Regular mowing and edging for a crisp, clean look
  • Seasonal fertilization and weed control programs
  • Aeration and overseeding for thicker, healthier grass
  • Leaf removal and complete property clean-ups

Irrigation Systems

Efficient irrigation is the backbone of a healthy landscape. We design and install smart watering systems that conserve water while keeping your plants thriving.

  • Custom sprinkler system design and professional installation
  • Drip irrigation for gardens, flower beds, and planters
  • Smart controller setup with weather-based scheduling
  • System maintenance, repairs, and winterization

Landscape Design

Transform your outdoor space into a stunning retreat with our custom landscape design services. From concept to completion, we bring your vision to life.

  • Custom garden design with carefully selected plant varieties
  • Hardscaping including patios, walkways, and retaining walls
  • Flower bed installation with seasonal color planning
  • Tree and shrub planting for year-round structure and beauty
