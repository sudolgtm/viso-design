What We Do

We take your yard and ideas and turn them into something you didn't even realize was possible.

Premium Okanagan Landscaping

Pinnacle Landscapes is one of the Okanagan's top landscape designers. With over 10 years of experience in both construction and design, we know what works and what doesn't. Every project is crafted with quality products and top-of-the-line workmanship.

We believe your yard should not just be the land around your house, but an extension of your home — a place for your kids to play, for you to relax, your sanctuary. Let's work together and bring your dreams to life.

Our Services

From concept to completion — full-service landscape design and construction

Landscape Design

Our ultimate goal is to take your yard and ideas and turn them into something you didn't even realize was possible. With years of expertise, we carefully design yards that will look great now and continue to do so as they grow.

  • Custom design consultation
  • 3D landscape visualization
  • Plant & material selection
  • Seasonal colour planning

Retaining Walls

Beautifully engineered retaining walls that add structure, function, and aesthetic appeal to your landscape. We use premium materials and expert techniques to ensure lasting durability.

  • Natural stone & interlocking block
  • Engineered drainage solutions
  • Tiered garden walls
  • Seat walls & planter integration

Water Features

Transform your outdoor space with the soothing sounds of custom water features. From tranquil ponds to elegant waterfalls, we create stunning aquatic focal points.

  • Custom ponds & waterfalls
  • Fountain installations
  • Pondless water features
  • Ecosystem-balanced designs

Paving Stones & Hardscaping

Create stunning patios, walkways, and entertaining spaces with premium paving stones. We select unique, exclusive products to ensure your yard stands out from the rest.

  • Patios & walkway installation
  • Driveway paving
  • Interlocking pavers
  • Custom stone patterns

Fire Features

Extend your outdoor living season with custom fire features. From intimate fire pits to grand outdoor fireplaces, we create warm gathering spaces for year-round enjoyment.

  • Custom fire pit designs
  • Outdoor fireplaces
  • Gas & wood-burning options
  • Seating integration
