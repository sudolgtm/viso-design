Our Services

Comprehensive electrical solutions for residential and commercial clients across the Okanagan Valley

Residential Services

Complete electrical solutions for your home. From new wiring and panel upgrades to lighting installation and safety corrections, we ensure your home is powered safely and efficiently. Our team treats every home with respect, leaving it clean and tidy after every job.

  • New home wiring and rough-ins
  • Panel upgrades and replacements
  • Smart home technology: Lutron, Google Nest, Sonos
  • Home safety inspections and wiring corrections

Commercial Services

Professional electrical contracting for businesses and commercial properties across the Okanagan. We specialize in electrical work for wineries, breweries, restaurants, and cafés — handling projects of any scale with minimal disruption, delivering code-compliant work on time and within budget.

  • Wineries, breweries, and restaurant electrical
  • Commercial tenant improvements
  • Office and retail wiring
  • Marina and boat dock electrical

Installations & Repairs

Expert installation and reliable repair services for all your electrical needs. Whether you need a new appliance wired in or a faulty circuit diagnosed, our experienced technicians respond quickly and get the job done right the first time.

  • Appliance and fixture installation
  • Electrical troubleshooting and repair
  • Breaker and fuse replacements
  • Emergency electrical service

Lighting Fixtures

Professional lighting installation, fixture upgrades, and custom lighting solutions to enhance the comfort and aesthetics of your space. We supply and install quality fixtures that match your style and budget — from indoor ambiance to outdoor landscape and pool lighting.

  • Indoor and outdoor lighting installation
  • Pool, hot tub, and landscape lighting
  • Fixture upgrades and replacements
  • Recessed, track, and security lighting

Renovation Wiring

Complete electrical wiring for home renovations and upgrades. We work alongside your renovation team to deliver seamless, code-compliant electrical work that integrates perfectly with your new space.

  • Kitchen and bathroom rewiring
  • Basement and addition wiring
  • New circuit installations
  • Renovation permit inspections

Service Upgrades

Electrical panel upgrades, service maintenance, and safety improvements. We bring older electrical systems up to modern standards, ensuring your home or business has the capacity to handle today's power demands.

  • 100 to 200 amp panel upgrades
  • Service entrance replacements
  • Surge protection installation
  • Electrical system maintenance

Wiring Corrections & Safety Improvements

We specialize in identifying and correcting hazardous wiring issues. Our team thoroughly inspects your electrical system and makes all necessary corrections to ensure your property is safe, code-compliant, and built to last.

  • Faulty wiring identification and repair
  • Code compliance upgrades
  • Safety inspection and testing
  • Older home rewiring services
