About Us

What We Do

We craft beautiful, functional outdoor spaces that our clients are proud to call their own.

Fractal Fern Landscape & Design is a Kelowna-based landscaping and arborist company dedicated to transforming ordinary yards into extraordinary outdoor living spaces. Founded on principles of honesty, communication, and craftsmanship, we take pride in every project we undertake — big or small.

From custom meditation gardens and stone pathways to expert tree care and complete property transformations, our team brings professional expertise, modern equipment, and genuine passion to every job site. We don't just build landscapes — we build lasting relationships with our clients.

Our Expertise

Services We Offer

Comprehensive landscaping and arborist solutions tailored to your property's unique needs.

Landscape Design & Installation

From concept to completion, we design and install outdoor spaces that reflect your personal style and enhance your property's natural beauty.

  • Custom landscape design tailored to your vision
  • Meditation gardens & tranquil retreat spaces
  • Drought-resistant planting & native vegetation
  • Complete property transformations

Arborist & Tree Care Services

Professional tree care and removal services to keep your property safe, healthy, and beautiful. Our certified arborist team handles it all.

  • Cedar pruning & tree trimming
  • Dead tree removal & hazard mitigation
  • Tree lowering & precision limb work
  • Fire hazard reduction & clearing

Yard Cleanup & Transformation

Revitalize neglected or overgrown properties with our comprehensive yard cleanup and transformation services.

  • Full property cleanup & debris removal
  • Ground leveling & site preparation
  • Weed removal & invasive plant control
  • Haul-away & worksite cleanup

Stone Pathways & Hardscaping

Enhance your outdoor space with beautiful, durable stone pathways, gravel work, and hardscaping features that stand the test of time.

  • Curved stone pathway installation
  • Gravel work & ground coverage
  • Mulch beds & planting areas
  • Custom hardscape features
