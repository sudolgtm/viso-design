About Us

Lethbridge's Trusted Roofing Professionals

Accent Roofing is a local company serving all of southern Alberta and southeastern British Columbia. Our number one goal is quality workmanship and customer satisfaction.

With the ever increasing extremities in the weather, we tar all shingles to assure there is never an issue from wind damage. We back this up with a 5 year installation guaranty and a 40 year guaranty on the shingles.
