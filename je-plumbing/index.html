What We Do

J & E Plumbing & Heating has been serving Kelowna for more than 25 years with quality workmanship and reasonable rates. We are a family-owned plumbing and heating company dedicated to providing honest, professional service for residential and commercial properties. Whether it's a burst pipe emergency, a furnace installation, or a full renovation, our team delivers reliable solutions you can count on.
