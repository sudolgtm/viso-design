What We Do

We transform outdoor spaces with expert craftsmanship and reliable service.

Planting Dreams Landscaping is a Chilliwack-based masonry and property maintenance company dedicated to quality workmanship and exceptional customer service. Whether you need a stunning brick facade, a durable retaining wall, a beautiful patio, or regular property upkeep, our experienced team delivers results that stand the test of time.

We bring over a decade of combined experience in the construction and landscaping industry, serving residential and commercial clients throughout Chilliwack and the Fraser Valley.

Our Services

Comprehensive masonry and property maintenance solutions for your home or business.

Masonry & Bricklaying

Expert masonry and bricklaying services for residential and commercial properties. We create durable, beautiful structures using premium materials and time-tested techniques.

  • Brick veneer and full brick construction
  • Stone facade and accent walls
  • Chimney repair and rebuild
  • Mortar repointing and restoration

Property Maintenance

Keep your property in top condition with our comprehensive maintenance services. We handle everything from seasonal cleanups to ongoing property care.

  • Seasonal yard cleanup and debris removal
  • Lawn care, hedge trimming, and pruning
  • Driveway and walkway sweeping
  • Gutter cleaning and downspout maintenance

Patio & Stonework

Transform your outdoor living space with custom patios and stonework. From natural stone pathways to elegant entertaining areas, we bring your vision to life.

  • Interlocking paver patios and walkways
  • Natural stone and flagstone installations
  • Outdoor kitchen and fire pit bases
  • Stepping stone paths and garden borders

Retaining Walls

Professional retaining wall construction for erosion control, landscaping, and structural support. We build walls that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing.

  • Segmental retaining wall systems
  • Natural stone and boulder walls
  • Timber and railroad tie retaining walls
  • Drainage solutions and erosion control
