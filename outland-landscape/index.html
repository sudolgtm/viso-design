What We Do

Transforming landscapes across the Okanagan with thoughtful, sustainable design

Landscape Architecture That Inspires

Outland Design is a full-service landscape architecture practice based in Kelowna, BC. We are committed to creating and sustaining great private and public spaces — from intimate residential gardens to large-scale community developments.

With expertise spanning urban design, masterplanning, and environmental stewardship, our team brings a collaborative approach to every project, ensuring each design reflects the unique character of its setting and the vision of its stakeholders.

Our Services

Comprehensive landscape architecture services tailored to your vision

Masterplanning

Comprehensive site planning and visioning that establishes the framework for successful development. We integrate site analysis, circulation, land use, and open space networks into cohesive master plans.

  • Site analysis and feasibility studies
  • Land use and circulation planning
  • Phasing and implementation strategies
  • Stakeholder and community engagement

Conceptual Landscape Design

Creative exploration of design possibilities translated into compelling visual concepts. We develop schematic designs that capture the spirit of a place while addressing functional requirements.

  • Schematic design and mood boards
  • 3D visualization and renderings
  • Planting plans and material selection
  • Hardscape and softscape integration

Green Roof & Vertical Garden Design

Innovative green infrastructure solutions that bring nature into the built environment. From extensive green roofs to living walls, we design systems that enhance biodiversity, manage stormwater, and improve air quality.

  • Extensive and intensive green roof systems
  • Living wall and vertical garden design
  • Stormwater management integration
  • Native plant selection and irrigation

Urban Design & Planning

Shaping memorable public realms through thoughtful urban design. We create vibrant streetscapes, plazas, and parks that foster community connection and support local identity.

  • Streetscape and public realm design
  • Park and open space planning
  • Municipal development approvals
  • Design guidelines and policy development
