Our Services

Full-Service Electrical Work

From small repairs to full renovations, we handle all your electrical needs with care, precision, and professional craftsmanship.

Residential Electrical

Complete electrical services for your home — from new construction wiring to panel upgrades and renovations. We ensure every job is safe, code-compliant, and built to last.

  • New construction wiring and rewiring
  • Electrical panel upgrades and replacements
  • Basement suite wiring for renovations
  • Lighting installation and outlet repairs

Commercial Electrical

Reliable electrical solutions for your business. We understand that downtime costs money — we work efficiently to get your commercial space powered and ready.

  • Cafe and restaurant electrical installations
  • Salon and retail space wiring
  • Commercial lighting and power solutions
  • New construction and renovation wiring

Electrical Inspections

Thorough home electrical inspections for insurance companies, older home assessments, and safety code compliance. We provide clear, honest reports you can rely on.

  • Insurance-required home inspections
  • Older home electrical safety assessments
  • Code compliance and safety checks
  • Detailed reports with recommendations

Emergency Service

When electrical emergencies strike, we respond fast. Available 24/7 for urgent electrical issues — because some problems can't wait until morning.

  • Fast response for urgent electrical issues
  • Power outage troubleshooting and repairs
  • Exposed wiring and hazard resolution
  • Available 24 hours, 7 days a week

Lighting Design & Installation

Transform your space with custom lighting solutions. From cozy indoor ambiance to bright outdoor security lighting, we help you design and install the perfect setup.

  • Indoor and outdoor lighting installation
  • Bathroom light and fan combinations
  • Energy-efficient LED upgrades
  • Custom lighting design recommendations

Troubleshooting & Repairs

Got an electrical problem? We diagnose the issue quickly and fix it right the first time. No guesswork, no unnecessary repairs — just honest, expert service.
