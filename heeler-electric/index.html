Electrical Services

Electrical Repairs & Panel Upgrades

Fast, reliable troubleshooting and repairs for all electrical issues. From flickering lights to full panel upgrades.

  • Electrical panel upgrades for increased capacity and safety
  • Service changes and breaker replacements
  • Emergency electrical troubleshooting

Lighting & Wiring

Professional lighting and wiring solutions for interior and exterior spaces, new builds, and renovations.

  • Interior and exterior lighting installation
  • Attic wiring and low-voltage systems
  • New circuit installation for renovations and additions
  • Landscape and accent lighting

Outlet & Switch Installation

New installations, upgrades, and relocations to meet modern electrical needs throughout your home.

  • New outlet and switch installation
  • GFCI and AFCI protected outlet upgrades
  • Outlet relocation for renovations and remodels
  • USB outlet and modern device integration

Code Compliance & Safety Inspections

All work meets current BC electrical codes with proper permits and inspections for your peace of mind.

  • Full electrical code compliance inspections
  • Permit handling and inspection coordination
  • Safety upgrades to meet current code requirements
  • Pre-purchase home electrical inspections

Kitchen & Bathroom Electrical

Specialized electrical work for kitchens and bathrooms, ensuring safety in wet areas.

  • Kitchen appliance circuit installation
  • Bathroom exhaust fan and lighting wiring
  • Heated floor thermostat installation
  • Wet-area GFCI protection compliance

Strata & Condo Electrical

In-suite upgrades and large-scale strata electrical maintenance with minimal disruption to residents.

  • In-suite electrical upgrades for condo owners
