What We Do

On The Top ROOFING LTD is a family-owned and operated roofing company serving Lethbridge, Medicine Hat, and surrounding areas since 2009. We specialize in residential and commercial roofing, siding, gutters, and exterior renovations.

Our owner-operators work on-site with our crews every day, ensuring every job meets our high standards. We use quality materials, offer fair pricing, and stand behind every project with a full warranty.

Our Services

From roof replacements to emergency repairs, we handle all your exterior needs with quality craftsmanship and reliable service.

Roof Installation & Shingling

We install high-quality asphalt shingle roofs that protect your home for decades. Whether you're building new or replacing an aging roof, our crew delivers precise, durable results.

  • Asphalt shingle roofing installation
  • Metal roofing systems
  • Flat & commercial roofing
  • Cedar & slate roofing

Roof Repair & Storm Damage

Lethbridge weather can be harsh on your roof. We respond quickly to leaks, wind damage, and storm-related issues to protect your home and give you peace of mind.

  • Emergency roof repair
  • Storm & wind damage restoration
  • Leak detection & repair
  • Shingle replacement & patching

Siding, Gutters & Exteriors

We install and repair Hardie Board siding, seamless gutters, and eavestroughs to keep your home's exterior looking beautiful and functioning properly through every season.

  • Hardie Board siding installation
  • Seamless gutter installation & repair
  • Eavestrough cleaning & maintenance
  • Downspout installation

Roof Inspection & Skylights

We provide thorough roof inspections using drone technology and professional-grade assessments. We also install and repair skylights to bring natural light into your home.

  • Drone-assisted roof inspections
  • Skylight installation & repair
  • Attic venting & insulation
  • Same-day digital quotes
