What We Do

Creating Beautiful, Functional Outdoor Living Spaces

MCG Landscaping Inc. is a West Kelowna-based landscape design and construction company dedicated to transforming ordinary yards into extraordinary outdoor environments. From concept to completion, our team combines creative vision with expert craftsmanship to deliver landscapes that inspire, function beautifully, and stand the test of time. Whether you're dreaming of a serene garden retreat, a modern entertaining space, or a complete property transformation, we bring your vision to life with precision and care.

Our Services

Complete Landscaping Services

From initial design to final installation, we handle every aspect of your outdoor transformation.

Landscape Design & Planning

Our design process begins with understanding your vision, lifestyle, and property. We create detailed landscape plans that balance aesthetics with functionality, ensuring every element serves a purpose.

  • Custom landscape architecture tailored to your property
  • 3D renderings and detailed site plans
  • Plant selection and garden layout optimization
  • Sustainable, low-maintenance design solutions

Garden Design & Zero-Scaping

Transform your yard with water-wise, low-maintenance landscaping that doesn't sacrifice beauty. Our zero-scaping solutions are perfect for homeowners looking to reduce water usage while maximizing curb appeal.

  • Drought-tolerant plant selection and xeriscaping
  • Front lawn replacement with stunning native gardens
  • Raised garden beds and ornamental planting
  • Seasonal color planning and perennial gardens

Hardscaping & Patio Design

Create the ultimate outdoor living space with custom hardscaping. From elegant patios to durable walkways, our masonry work adds structure, style, and lasting value to your property.

  • Custom patio design and installation
  • Concrete masonry and decorative stonework
  • Driveway and walkway landscaping
  • Retaining walls and grading solutions

Fire Pits & Outdoor Living

Extend your living season with custom fire features and outdoor living elements. Whether you want a cozy fire pit for cool Okanagan evenings or a complete outdoor kitchen, we design spaces that become the heart of your home.

  • Custom fire pit and fireplace installations
  • Outdoor kitchen and entertainment areas
  • Integrated lighting and ambiance design
  • Complete outdoor room transformations
