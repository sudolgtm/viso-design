Our Plumbing Services

Comprehensive plumbing solutions for Kelowna homes and businesses — available 24/7

Service 01

Leak Detection & Repair

Hidden leaks can cause serious damage to your property. We use advanced techniques to quickly locate and repair leaks of all types — from dripping faucets to hidden slab leaks.

  • Accurate leak detection technology
  • Pipe repair and replacement
  • Emergency leak response
  • Water damage prevention
Service 02

Hot Water Tank Installation & Repair

From emergency replacements to routine maintenance, we handle all makes and models of hot water tanks. We'll help you choose the right size and energy-efficient option for your home.

  • New tank installation
  • Emergency tank replacement
  • Routine maintenance & flushing
  • Energy-efficient upgrades
Service 03

Drain Cleaning & Repair

Slow drains and stubborn clogs are more than an inconvenience — they can lead to bigger plumbing issues. Our professional drain cleaning restores flow and prevents future problems.

  • Kitchen & bathroom drain clearing
  • Sewer line cleaning & inspection
  • Clogged drain repair
  • Preventive maintenance
Service 04

Faucet & Pipe Repair

Dripping faucets, low water pressure, and noisy pipes are signs it's time for professional attention. We repair and replace faucets, pipes, and fixtures throughout your home.

  • Faucet repair & replacement
  • Pipe repair & repiping
  • Fixture installation
  • Water pressure solutions
Service 05

Backflow Prevention & Testing

Protect your drinking water with professional backflow prevention services. We install, test, and maintain backflow devices to ensure your water supply stays clean and safe.

  • Backflow device installation
  • Annual testing & certification
  • Commercial backflow solutions
  • Code compliance & repairs
Service 06

24/7 Emergency Plumbing

Plumbing emergencies don't wait for business hours — and neither do we. Whether it's a burst pipe in the middle of the night or a weekend water heater failure, we're here when you need us most.

  • 24-hour emergency response
  • Burst pipe repair
  • Sewage backup cleanup
  • Same-day service guaranteed
