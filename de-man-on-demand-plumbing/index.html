Your Trusted Plumbing Professionals

At de Man On Demand Plumbing Ltd., we're your licensed plumbing professionals offering complete plumbing solutions for residential and light commercial clients across Kelowna and surrounding areas. Our Plumbing Services

From emergency repairs to full renovations, we handle all your plumbing needs with expertise and care.

Plumbing Repairs & Drain Cleaning

From dripping faucets to clogged drains, our licensed plumbers quickly diagnose and resolve any plumbing issue. We use professional-grade tools and proven techniques to get your plumbing back in top shape.

  • Fixtures and faucet replacement
  • Drain cleaning and video inspections
  • Pipe repair and re-piping services
  • Fixture and appliance installation

Hot Water Heating Services

With specialized expertise in hot water systems, we install, repair, and maintain all types of water heating equipment. Whether you need a new tank or a high-efficiency boiler, we have the skills to get it done right.

  • Hot water tank installation and repair
  • Boiler repair and maintenance
  • Tankless water heater installation
  • Heating system troubleshooting

Renovations & Installations

Planning a renovation? We work alongside you to handle all plumbing aspects of your project. From kitchen and bathroom remodels to new appliance hookups, our team ensures seamless integration with your existing systems.

  • Kitchen and bathroom plumbing renovations
  • New construction plumbing
  • Appliance installation (washing machines, dishwashers)
  • Residential and light commercial projects

Gas Lines & Emergency Services

Our licensed professionals handle all gas line installations and repairs with the utmost attention to safety. From earthquake valves to gas appliance hookups, we ensure your gas systems are code-compliant and secure.

  • Gas line installation and repair
  • Earthquake valve installation
  • Gas appliance hookups
  • Emergency plumbing services
