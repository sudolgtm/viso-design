Our Services

Complete Roofing Solutions

From emergency repairs to brand-new installations, we handle every aspect of your roofing needs with precision and care.

Roof Repairs

Whether it's storm damage, a persistent leak, or worn-out shingles, we respond quickly with expert repairs that restore your roof's integrity. Our team assesses the damage thoroughly and provides lasting solutions.

  • Leak detection and repair
  • Storm damage restoration
  • Shingle and tile replacement
  • Emergency tarp and board-up services

New Roof Installations

Building a new home or need a complete roof replacement? We install durable, high-quality roofing systems tailored to your property's style and the Okanagan climate. We work with top-tier materials to ensure long-lasting protection.

  • Residential roof installation
  • Commercial roofing systems
  • Asphalt shingles and metal roofing
  • Flat roof and low-slope solutions

Roof Inspections

Regular inspections catch small problems before they become costly repairs. Our thorough roof inspections identify hidden damage, assess overall condition, and provide you with a clear picture of your roof's health.

  • Pre-purchase roof inspections
  • Seasonal maintenance checks
  • Insurance claim assessments
  • Drone-aided roof surveys
