Our Services

Comprehensive roofing solutions for residential and commercial properties in Kelowna and the Okanagan.

Roofing Installation & Repair

From new roof installations to comprehensive repairs, we handle all types of roofing projects with precision and care. Our team uses quality materials and proven techniques to ensure your roof stands the test of time.

  • Complete roof installations for new constructions
  • Expert roof repairs and leak remediation
  • Asphalt shingle, metal, and flat roofing systems
  • Gutter installation and maintenance

Chimney Sealing & Repair

Protect your home from water damage and drafts with our professional chimney sealing and repair services. We ensure your chimney is structurally sound and properly sealed against the elements.

  • Chimney crown repair and sealing
  • Flashing installation and replacement
  • Masonry repair and tuckpointing
  • Waterproofing and weatherproofing solutions

Commercial Roofing

We provide comprehensive commercial roofing solutions for businesses in Kelowna. Our commercial team understands the unique demands of business properties and delivers durable, code-compliant roofing systems.

  • Flat roof installation and repair
  • Commercial re-roofing and upgrades
  • Low-slope roofing systems (TPO, EPDM, PVC)
  • Preventative maintenance programs

Emergency Roof Repairs

When disaster strikes, Ogopogo Roofing is here to help. We offer prompt emergency roof repair services to protect your property from further damage. Available 24/7 for urgent roofing needs.

  • Storm damage repair and tarping
  • Leak detection and emergency patching
  • Fallen tree and debris damage repair
  • 24/7 rapid response for urgent situations
