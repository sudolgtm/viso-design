What We Do

Sun Valley Tree & Lawn Care is a Kelowna-based landscaping company dedicated to making your property look its best.

Owned and operated by Wes, Sun Valley Tree & Lawn Care has been serving the Kelowna area for over 4 years. We specialize in lawn maintenance, tree care, yard cleanup, and commercial landscaping — delivering high-quality results at competitive prices.


Our team is known for being reliable, hardworking, and professional. We show up on time, get the job done efficiently, and leave your property looking beautiful. Whether it's a one-time cleanup or ongoing maintenance, we've got you covered.
