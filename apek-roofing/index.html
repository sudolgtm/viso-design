What We Do

Apek Roofing is your premier choice for all your roofing needs in Lethbridge and Southern Alberta. With a team of highly skilled professionals, we offer top-notch services including roof installation, repairs, and maintenance for both residential and commercial properties.

Our commitment to quality craftsmanship ensures that your roof is built to last. We use premium materials and advanced techniques to deliver outstanding results that exceed your expectations, all while providing personalized solutions tailored to your specific needs and budget.

Our Roofing Services

From new installations to repairs and replacements, we handle all your roofing needs with expert craftsmanship and premium materials.

Roof Installations

Expert roof installation services for new residential and commercial properties. Our skilled team uses premium materials and industry-leading techniques to ensure a durable, long-lasting roof that enhances your property's value.

  • Premium asphalt shingle installation
  • Cedar shake roofing systems
  • SBS torch-down flat roofing
  • Custom solutions for any property type

Roof Replacements

Complete roof replacement services that transform your old, worn-out roof into a sturdy, aesthetically pleasing upgrade. We handle the entire process from tear-off to final inspection, ensuring minimal disruption to your daily life.

  • Full tear-off and disposal of old roof
  • Wide selection of premium materials
  • Expert installation with manufacturer warranties
  • Thorough site cleanup after completion

Shingle Repair

Damaged shingles? Don't let it compromise the integrity of your roof. Our skilled technicians assess the damage, provide efficient repairs, and ensure that your shingles are secure and weather-resistant.

  • Expert assessment of shingle damage
  • Fast, reliable shingle replacement
  • Weather-resistant repair solutions
  • Matching materials for seamless results

Roof Leak Repair

Don't let a leaky roof cause costly damage to your property. Our team quickly identifies the source of leaks and provides effective, long-lasting repairs to protect your home or business from water damage.

  • Thorough leak detection and diagnosis
  • Fast emergency repair response
  • Permanent fixes, not temporary patches
  • Prevention of mold and structural damage
