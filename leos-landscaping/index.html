What We Do

Complete Landscaping Services for Chilliwack Homes & Properties

Leo's Landscaping Ltd. is a family-owned business with over 15 years of experience transforming and maintaining outdoor spaces throughout Chilliwack and the Fraser Valley. We take pride in delivering dependable, high-quality landscaping services at fair prices.

From regular lawn maintenance to custom landscape design, our team provides reliable, thorough service that keeps your property looking its best all year round.

Our Services

Professional Landscaping Services

Comprehensive outdoor care tailored to your property's needs — from regular upkeep to complete transformations.

Lawn Mowing & Cutting

Keep your lawn looking pristine with our reliable mowing and cutting services. We handle properties of all sizes with professional-grade equipment for a clean, even cut every time.

  • Regular weekly and bi-weekly mowing schedules
  • Professional-grade equipment for clean cuts
  • Lawn dressing and raccoon damage repair
  • Edging and trimming for a polished finish

Hedge Trimming & Tree Pruning

Maintain healthy, attractive hedges and trees with our expert trimming and pruning services. Our team ensures your greenery stays shapely, vibrant, and well-maintained throughout the seasons.

  • Hedge trimming and shaping for all varieties
  • Tree pruning for health and aesthetics
  • Seasonal clean-up and deadwood removal
  • Promoting healthy growth and flowering

Landscape Design & Rock Gardens

Transform your outdoor space with custom landscape design and beautiful rock gardens. Whether you want a complete yard makeover or a stunning new garden feature, we bring your vision to life.

  • Custom rock garden design and construction
  • Landscape planning and yard transformations
  • Natural stone and decorative rock installation
  • Prompt project completion, even on short notice

Complete Property Maintenance

From weed control to power washing, our comprehensive property maintenance packages keep your entire outdoor space in top condition. We provide reliable, thorough service you can count on year-round.

  • Weed control and prevention treatments
  • Power washing for driveways and walkways
  • Spring and fall property cleanups
  • Flexible seasonal scheduling and timing
